Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about buying, selling, and investing with NexaHomes.

How do I schedule a viewing?

You can schedule a viewing by visiting the property page and clicking "Schedule Viewing," or by contacting our concierge team directly through the Contact page. We offer both in-person and virtual tours to suit your schedule.

Do you offer international property listings?

Yes, NexaHomes specializes in luxury properties across the globe. Our "Global Reach" network ensures we have exclusive access to listings in major international markets including London, Dubai, Paris, and Tokyo.

What services do you provide for sellers?

We provide a comprehensive suite of services for sellers, including professional photography and videography, targeted global marketing campaigns, private showings, and expert negotiation strategies to maximize your property's value.

Is there a registration fee to join NexaHomes?

No, creating an account with NexaHomes is completely free. Registered users gain access to saved searches, exclusive "off-market" listings, and personalized property recommendations.

How can I finance a luxury property purchase?

We work with a network of trusted financial advisors and private banks who specialize in high-value real estate transactions. We can connect you with the right experts to structure your financing efficiently.
